Healthcare Thinks Lean

Lauren Mock

Healthcare has much to learn, from of all places, the automotive industry. Learn how ideas conceived on the Toyota automotive assembly line in post-war Japan is revolutionizing healthcare delivery.

Drinking Drugs: Pharmaceuticals in Our Tap Water

Gretchen Holm

When you are thirsty, drink some water. Take some drugs. What?! You will never look at a glass of water the same way again!

Stem Cell Research Targets Cartilage Repair

Nancy Organ

The 27 million Americans suffering from osteoarthritis, a chronic condition characterized by painful inflammation of the joints, may soon see relief. Research in stem cells may provide novel solutions that repair or regenerate damaged tissue.
