Spring 2007 Semester Info

Students may earn an optional 2 units of Pass/No Pass credit upon successful completion of the course. There are no prerequisites for students; the only requirement is for students to be prepared and eager to participate in class.


Students may sign up for this course during Phase 1 and 2 of Telebears and as well as at the beginning of the new semester. The Course Control Numbers (CCN) are divided into upper and lower divisions, but students may sign up for whichever one they are eligible for.


MCB 98 CCN for lower-division classmen: 57892


MCB 198 CCN for upper-division classmen: 59239


Classes meet Tuesdays 5-7 pm at 141 Giannini


Please note: ISSUES, Berkeley Medical Journal begins the third week of classes (Jan. 30th).
